Safely Guiding the Immune System to Fight Disease

Eradivir is developing drugs that bind to infected cells, serving as a beacon to guide the immune system.

A new Paradigm in Disease Targeting.

Much like precision guided munitions, our patented drug delivery technology applies medicine to the specific sites in the body where it is needed. This improves efficacy, reduces side effects, and lowers required dosage.

Our Company

One Technology,
Many Targets

Our platform technology allows for rapid iteration. By adding in disease-specific targeting ligands late in synthesis, we can quickly adapt to a wide variety of disease states. This has allowed us to develop a robust pipeline of potent drug candidates that bind to a variety of proteins unique to a number of prominent maladies.

Rethinking Influenza

Our lead molecule, EV25, has been shown to be more effective than the current standard of care against all strains of influenza. EV25 functions by linking infected cells to two anti-hapten antibodies, thereby recruiting the body's innate immune system to rapidly clear the disease.
